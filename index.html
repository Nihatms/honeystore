Types

Clover Honey

As the name suggests, bees make this type of honey by collecting nectar from clover (Trifolium) flowers.There are approximately 300 species of clover plants.

Wildflower Honey

Wildflower honey is a polyfloral honey made when bees pollinate a wide variety of flowers and blossoms. As such, its quality and flavor vary greatly depending on the composition of wildflowers involved in its natural production.

Acacia Honey

Also known as locust honey, is derived from the nectar of the Robinia pseudoacacia flower.Acacia honey may aid wound healing, improve acne, and offer additional benefits due to its powerful antioxidants.
